All about Dogs

The dog is a domesticated carnivoran of the family Canidae. It is part of the wolf-like canids, and is the most widely abundant terrestrial carnivore.

What to know about Dogs


  • Gestation period: 58 – 68 days
  • Daily sleep: 12 – 14 hours (Adult)
  • Lifespan: 10 – 13 years
  • They are adorable
  • Family: Canidae
  • Scientific name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Height: 6 – 44 in. (At Shoulder)
